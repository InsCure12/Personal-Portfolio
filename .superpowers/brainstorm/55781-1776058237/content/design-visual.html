Section 2: Visual Polish & Typography

Elevating the look from good to premium. Here's what I propose.

Typography

Current

System fonts only — no custom font loaded. Looks generic and inconsistent across platforms.

Proposed

Inter for everything — headings and body. Clean, technical, developer-friendly. Variable font for optimal loading. Consistent cross-platform rendering.

Scale: 12 / 14 / 16 (base) / 18 / 24 / 32 / 48

Weights: 400 (body) / 500 (labels) / 600 (subheadings) / 700 (headings)

Spacing Refinement

  • Enforce consistent 8px spacing grid across all components
  • Section vertical padding: 80px (desktop) → 48px (mobile)
  • Card padding standardized to 24px
  • Component gaps aligned to 8 / 16 / 24 / 32 / 48 scale
